Opening Ceremony x Vans Authentic LX ‘Paisley’ Pack


Opening Ceremony has teamed up with Vans Vault once again. This time around they have a new ‘Paisley’ Pack which is highlighted with two color options of the These two Vans Authentic LX releases is highlighted with paisley wrapping the entire shoes which gives both an interesting yet unique look. Looking closer, one is done in Tango Red and Black while the other features Calliste Green and Black. Following we have canvas on the liner while both are completed with a rubber waffle outsole.Continue to scroll below to check out more images of the Opening Ceremony x Vans Authentic Paisley Pack which will give you a closer look. First Look: Supreme x Vans ‘Blood and Semen’ Pack


Supreme and Vans relationship will continue with a brand new collection that is expected to launch soon. Shown is the new Supreme x Vans ‘Blood and Semen’ Pack which consists of three models. Those models includes the Vans Old Skool, Vans Chukka and Vans SK8-Hi. Each from the collection features ‘Blood and Semen’ which was designed by artist Andres Serrano who has worked with metal bands such as Metallica and Godflesh.At the time of writing, a release date for the Supreme Vans Blood and Semen Pack has yet to be announced. However once we have additional information, we will make sure to update you. Continue to scroll below to check out more images which will provide you with a closer look. Vans ‘Japan Indigo’ Collection


Vans is now starting to release a new exclusive Japan Indigo Collection. Apart of the lineup is the Vans Old Skool, Vans SK8-Hi and the Vans Slip-On which is the brands most popular silhouettes. All three models are constructed with flannel brushed fabric across the uppers while ‘Japan Blue’ and White form a checkered pattern. Finishing the look is White used once again, this time on the laces, panel branding, midsole and outsole.At the time of writing, the Vans Japan Indigo Collection is now starting to show up at select Vans retailers in Japan. Currently we don’t have release information for a stateside launch but once we have more details, we will make sure to update you.
